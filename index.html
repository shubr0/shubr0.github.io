About Me

With a background in mechanical engineering and robotics from KAIST, I specialize in bringing robots from the research lab to real-world production environments. My work spans autonomous mobile robots (AMRs), industrial manipulators, wearable exoskeletons, and teleoperation systems.

Currently at Crazing Lab, I architect AMR control platforms for smart-factory automation. Previously, as a founding engineer at Autogeared, I helped deploy robotic kitchen systems in 10+ restaurants within 12 months.
